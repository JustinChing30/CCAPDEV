What is Green Tech?

This forum application is designed for students of the De La Salle University College of Computer Studies (CCS). It serves as a space where CCS students can freely ask questions, seek academic help, or simply voice out their thoughts. Whether you're struggling with a subject, want to rant about a tough week, or share helpful resources, this platform is for you. It encourages open conversations and peer support within the CCS community. Students can interact through posts and comments, building a sense of camaraderie beyond the classroom. The forum is safe, student-friendly, and made to foster honest expression. We believe every CCS student deserves a space to be heard.
